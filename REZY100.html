REZY100 - For Travel Management Companies
Rezy100 is an all-in-one reservation system and business solution for travel managers supported by a powerful booking engine with access to GDS providers, hotel aggregators, car rentals, activity bookings and more to give your business anadvantage over competition. Rezy100 is a single contact point for travel agents and tour operators to access all-inclusive travel and tour options including flights, hotels, car rentals, activities, airport lounge passes, transportation and more. Rezy100 is a fully functional reservation and business management system that allows users to compete with the giants of the trade. The platform comes with preloaded travel content and built-in payment gateway.
Existing modules also allow users to add their own proprietary inventory including essential information such as descriptions, requirements, restrictions, age limits, check-in and check-out times, images, ratings, taxes and more. Rezy100 is the perfect asset to any travel agency looking for the latest tool in travel management. Rezy100 is great for:
  • TRAVEL MANAGERS
  • TRAVEL AGENTS
  • TOUR OPERATORS
  • TOURISM BOARDS
