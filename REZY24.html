REZY24 - For Travel Service Providers
Rezy24 is an end-to-end solution for businesses in the travel, tourism and hospitality industries.Rezy24 empowers businesses to create and manage inventory, update pricing, keep in contact with clients and offer a B2C booking option on their website. The system updates in real-time for efficient reservations tracking and management with a secure built-in payment gateway. With Rezy24, customers will be able to include a user’s business in their travels when they confidently book online knowing they have reservations. The simply designed but technologically powerful service is a cloud-based platform.
Update inventory, track sales or offer deals on the go, at home or at the office. The fully developed inventory module allows users to update, add and maintain inventory including descriptions, requirements, restrictions, age limits, check-in and check-out times, images, ratings, taxes and more. Rezy24 also provides access to TravelsBond and HYE:BYE. Activity providers, travel service providers and transfer services can add their inventory to HYEBYE.com, a B2C lifestyle marketplace. These providers as well as hoteliers and airlines are granted access to TravelsBond, reaching travel management companies around the world.
